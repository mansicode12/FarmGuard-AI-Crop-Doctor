Explore common farming questions and expert-backed solutions from our community.

Q: How to treat tomato leaf spot naturally?

A: Try neem oil spray mixed with water every 5 days. It’s effective for mild infections.

Q: When should I irrigate wheat during summer?

A: Wheat needs irrigation during CRI, booting, and grain filling stages.

Q: Any recommended apps for weather alerts for farmers?

A: Use FarmGuard’s built-in weather feature or apps like IMD Mausam.

Q: Can I plant maize right after harvesting rice?

A: Yes! It’s called sequential cropping. Replenish nutrients before sowing.
